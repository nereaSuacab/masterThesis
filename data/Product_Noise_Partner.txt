B&K 2245 sound level meter with the Product Noise Partner concept combines ease of use and operational simplicity with a complete product noise measurement toolset so that the system's potential can be harnessed by any user, from novice to acoustics specialist.

Together with an B&K 2245 sound level meter, Product Noise Partner is a complete, focused toolset for product noise testing – from sound power determination according to international standards, to verifying safe noise emissions from toys according to EN 71-1. B&K 2245 sound level meter with the Product Noise Partner concept combines ease of use and operational simplicity with a complete product noise measurement toolset so that the system's potential can be harnessed by any user, from novice to acoustics specialist.
The Product Noise Partner app measures sound power levels for machinery and equipment such as household appliances in order to comply with national and international regulations and help promote a safe and healthier environment.
MOBILE APP
With the Product Noise Partner app downloaded to your phone from the App Store
®
,the sound level meter can connect to your mobile phone via Wi-Fi
®
and be operated away from the sound field via an unparalleled mobile-app control interface. An intuitive 3D geometry view ensures that you measure all positions without mistakes, and final test results and pass/fail indications are shown instantly on the app when measurements are complete. Additionally, customizable metadata, pictures, text and voice notes, and videos can be embedded with the measurements.
PC APP
The Product Noise Partner PC application supports you through every step of the measurement process, from choosing a standard, through defining the device geometry and measurement positions, to calculating the final result. The PC app can even be used to wirelessly control measurements with your B&K 2245. Product Noise Partner PC software is preconfigured with the tools needed for storing, reviewing and reporting your data.

USER SCENARIOS
Measuring and reporting of sound power levels in accordance with ISO 3744 and ISO 3746 standards to demonstrate product compliance with noise specifications for legislation or commendation
Assessing noise emissions from toys according to EN 71-1
Determining noise emissions of machinery and equipment for ensuring compliance with standards or benchmarking for comparisons
Determining sound pressure levels at workstations
R&D and quality assurance testing products for analysis to identify and evaluate prominent discrete tones and impulsive noise
